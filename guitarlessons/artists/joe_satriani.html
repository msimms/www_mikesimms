Joe Satriani

Gear

Joe Satriani plays js1000 and js2000 ibanez guitars that are typically outfitted with his signature dimarzio pickups. Over the years he has used marshall and peavey amplifiers and he is reported to use or have used the following effects pedals:
  • Dunlop CryBaby Wah
  • Boss Distortion
  • Fulltone Ultimate Octave
  • Digitech Whammy
  • Boss Super Chorus
  • Boss Delay
  • Chandler Digital Eelay
  • Electro-Harmonix POG (polyphonic octave generator) - on the album Super Colossal
A schematic of one rig that he has used can be found at Guitar Geek.

Playing Style

Pitch Axis Theory

Satriani is well known for his use of Pitch Axis Theory a technique common in progressive rock.

Speed

He achieves his speed primarily through the use of hammer ons and pull offs.
