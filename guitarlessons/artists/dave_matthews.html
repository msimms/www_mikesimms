Dave Matthews

Gear

Dave Matthews is known primarily as an acoustic guitarist. His gear typically consists of Martin and Taylor acoustic guitars. His primary guitar is a Martin dm3md.

Playing Style

Dave's style is in large part due to his preference for some unusual chord voicing. Of particular note is the following chord sometimes referred to as the Dave Matthews Chord. The chord is comprised of a root note typically played on the sixth string combined with a third or minor third and it's octave.
